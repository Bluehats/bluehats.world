We promise to keep the BlueHats newsletters short and focused on the
most important news about FLOSS in the public sector.

-   [Schleswig-Holstein](https://interoperable-europe.ec.europa.eu/collection/open-source-observatory-osor/news/new-open-innovation-and-open-source-strategy-schleswig-holstein) (GER), [Aarhus and Copenhagen](https://interoperable-europe.ec.europa.eu/collection/open-source-observatory-osor/news/aarhus-and-copenhagen-choose-open-source) (DK), [Lyon](https://interoperable-europe.ec.europa.eu/collection/open-source-observatory-osor/news/municipality-lyon-moves-towards-open-source) (FR) move to Free Software.
-   [Barcelona is the first city in the world to endorse the UN Open Source Principles](https://www.barcelona.cat/infobarcelona/en/barcelona-is-the-first-city-in-the-world-to-endorse-the-un-open-source-principles_1567058.html), October 2025
-   The EU Commission published a webpage on [Cyber Resilience Act - Open source](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/policies/cra-open-source), December 2025
-   European Member States signed the [Declaration for European Digital Sovereignty](https://assets.innovazione.gov.it/1763560898-declaration_on_digital_sovereignty.pdf), November 2025.
-   The [OSPO Alliance launched FLOSS PSO](https://interoperable-europe.ec.europa.eu/collection/open-source-observatory-osor/news/ospo-alliance-launched-new-volunteer-driven-network), a network of public sector OSPOs, November 2025

-   developer.overheid.nl published [recommendations for the government's Git forge](https://developer.overheid.nl/blog/2025/11/11/git-forge-overheid), November 2025
